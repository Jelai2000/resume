About me

I am a passionate and results-driven IT professional with a strong foundation in Web development and IT support. With a background in Information Technology, I possess a solid grasp of various programming languages including HTML5, JavaScript, C++, and Java, along with hands-on experience in database management using MySQL. My skills extend to website design and maintenance, leveraging tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Figma, and Bootstrap to create visually appealing and responsive user interfaces.

During my internships and work experience at The TileExpo Trading Inc., Chrislmm Sentimo Kumon, and Connected Technology, LLC, I have honed my problem-solving abilities and adaptability in IT support, Web development, and network monitoring.

I am committed to continuous learning and stay updated with the latest trends and technologies to ensure the best solutions for every challenge. As a proactive communicator and a team collaborator, I thrive in environments that foster innovation and creativity.

My personal portfolio and project experience reflect my ability to transform concepts into dynamic, user-friendly solutions. I am eager to bring my expertise in both technical and soft skills to a forward-thinking team.
